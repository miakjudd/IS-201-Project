Artistic Philosophy

Dance is more than a series of turns and leaps; it is a bridge that connects souls. In 2024, as an international soloist in California, I found that even when language failed, dance allowed me to connect deeply with performers from across the globe.

My mission is to share stories that are too complex for words, using the stage as a space for therapy, connection, and fulfillment. Whether on the national stage with the Cougarettes or at a local camp for children, I believe movement is the ultimate form of truth.
